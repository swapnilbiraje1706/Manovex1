Turn online articles into speed reading sessions

Online articles are surrounded by navigation, ads, sidebars, popups, and related links. An article speed reader removes that clutter and keeps attention on the text.

Why web pages slow reading

The problem is not always the article. It is the environment around it. Notifications, recommendations, and visual clutter constantly invite the reader to leave the page.

Clean text creates a better session

When the article is extracted into plain readable text, it can be paced like a document. The reader can choose WPM, use focus mode, and finish without scanning a noisy page.

Use pasted text when import fails

Some websites block automatic extraction. A good tool should still let the user paste copied article text and read it in the same focused interface.
