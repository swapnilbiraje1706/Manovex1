How to use an EPUB speed reader

EPUB files are built for reflowable text, which makes them a strong format for browser-based speed reading.

Why EPUB works well

Unlike fixed-page PDFs, EPUB books usually store text in chapters. That makes extraction cleaner and helps the reader move into a focused display without page-layout distractions.

Use WPM by chapter type

A familiar self-help chapter may be comfortable at a higher speed. A dense technical chapter may need slower WPM. Chapter-by-chapter control helps users avoid one-speed reading.

Protect long-book momentum

Many people buy books but do not finish them. A speed reader helps by creating short, repeatable sessions and removing the feeling that each page is a separate task.
